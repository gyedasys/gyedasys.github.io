    • Who we are

    ASASSD club is a project of Copiange Education Initiative (CEI) a non Governmental Organisation founded in 2007 and fully registered in 2010 with the government of Uganda.

    Early March 2013, it was resolved that ASASSD Club be initiated in secondary schools in Rubirizi district under Copiange Education Initiative (CEI) a non-Governmental Organization. ASASSD’s formation was as a result of the need to help and guide secondary school students on the consequences of early Sex, drinking Alcohol, Smoking, Strikes and the use of Drugs while at school! The Club encourages students to put more efforts in academics and discipline through career guidance counseling with a team of well-trained and qualified counselors.

    Avoid playing Sex, Alcohol, Smoking, Strikes and Drugs is a secondary school club dedicated to seeing students empowered in their struggle for bad acts against the background of widespread actions in schools such as;    »»Pre-marital Sex
       »»Alcohol
       »»Smoking
       »»Strikes
       »»Drugs

    Avoidance of the above acts by students will lead to good quality education, moral upright citizens, principled and hardworking future persons, integral persons, role models and motivate speakers as well as outstanding future leaders of this nation.

    Core Strategic Objectives

      1. Uplifting students’ talents in areas of drama, debate, essay writing, writing articles, singing and dancing songs, sports
      2. Helping students; to keeping their virginities till marriage through sensitizing them about the dangers of early Sex, drinking Alcohol, Smoking various types of cigarettes, Strikes and use of drugs at a tender-age.
      3. Interaction during ASASSD Club events (internal and external) through sharing personal life experiences and testimonies, this comforts and relieves them thus bringing hope and transformation to students.

    Our Principles

    ASASSD Club is anchored on principles of Reliability, Integrity, Knowledge, and Charity, Voluntary work at school, Success, Love, Religious morals and social transparency.

    Thematic Areas

    “ASASSD” exists to deal with the core thematic areas of Pre-marital Sex, Use of drugs, Drinking Alcohol, Smoking Cigarettes and opium. We appreciate the challenges presented in these areas and we lay out our course of action.

    Drinking Alcohol

    Students have taken an advantage of packed spirits (beck ham, coffee spirit, zebra, royal-vodka) of which they illegally sneak into schools with the help of day-scholars or gatekeepers thus consuming them in bathrooms and toilets at their appropriate time and dump the sachets in the latrines. If one is caught drinking or in-possession of alcohol immediately he/she is expelled.

    Smoking Cigarettes and opium

    Some students begin smoking cigarettes (sportsman, super match, Rex, safari) and opium as a joke! Little did they know it’s out-come as an addiction; with well-trained Counselors, students are properly guided and sensitized on the consequences of smoking thus transforming them into better persons.

    Use of drugs

    A number of students are victims of drugs which are so harmful to their health. Drugs like marijuana and cocaine are mostly used by students for purposes of long-hours revision, acquiring more knowledge, talents in both singing and dancing which is a pure superstition!

    Strikes

    Strikes have become so rampant in secondary schools: in fact 2012, 2nd term is a good example that strikes became a national issue! Some of the Strikes are as a result of smoking, drinking alcohol, love affairs, poor feeding, and poor means of administration among others that affect the schools’ performance.

    Our Vision

    “enable students have a prosperous future through avoiding early Sex, Alcohol, Smoking, Strikes and Drugs”

    Our Motto
