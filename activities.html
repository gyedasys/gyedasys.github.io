    • Our Activities

    We hold activities that aim at uplifting students’ talents in areas of drama, debate, essay writing, writing articles, singing and dancing songs, sports.

    Debates

    On Saturday June 28, 2014, ASASSD Club, Kisoko High School in Tororo District, hosted a well thought-out an educative debate whose motion “The spread of AIDS in Uganda must be blamed on women”. Tororo Girl’s school, Majansi High school, Tororo Town College, Great Aubrey Memorial College, Rubongi S.S and Tororo Progressive Academy schools fully participated. Tororo Girls’ School emerged winners and was given ASASSD trophy whereas Rubongi S.S the second with two charcoal iron boxes by General Manager NWSC Tororo –branch Mr. Charles Okuonzi the Chief Guest.

    Talks

    The Club encourages students to put more efforts in academics and discipline through career guidance counseling with a team of well-trained and qualified counselors. Interaction during ASASSD Club events (internal and external) through sharing personal life experiences and testimonies, this comforts and relieves them thus bringing hope and transformation to students.

    Drama

    In April 2013, Ryeru Girls hosted ASASSD Drama competitions; five schools attended and participated fully. Ryeru Boys emerged winners with ASASSD Trophy and Kichwamba High School emerged 2nd with an electric flat iron as their prizes.
